Adidas

Adidas Logo

Adidas AG is a German multinational corporation that designs and manufactures shoes, clothing, and accessories. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe and the second-largest in the world.

History

Adidas was founded in 1949 by Adolf Dassler, who had previously worked for his brother Rudolf Dassler at the Gebr√ºder Dassler Schuhfabrik. The company's name is a portmanteau of "Adi" (Adolf's nickname) and "Das" (short for Dassler).

Products

Adidas' product line includes footwear, apparel, and accessories for a wide range of sports and activities, including soccer, basketball, running, and training. The company is known for its iconic three-stripe design, which is featured on many of its products.

Notable Products

  • Stan Smith sneakers: A classic tennis shoe that has been popular since the 1970s.
  • Superstar sneakers: A basketball shoe that was first released in 1969 and has been a staple of hip-hop culture since the 1980s.
  • Predator soccer cleats: A line of football boots that are designed for power and precision.

Sustainability

Adidas is committed to reducing its environmental impact and has set a goal of using only recycled polyester in its products by 2024. The company has also partnered with Parley for the Oceans to turn ocean plastic into high-performance sportswear.
