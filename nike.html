Nike

Nike Logo

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services.

History

Nike was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports. The company changed its name to Nike, Inc. in 1971 and introduced its iconic "Just Do It" slogan in 1988. Nike is named after the Greek goddess of victory, Nike.

Products

Nike's product line includes footwear, apparel, and equipment for a wide range of sports and activities, including running, basketball, soccer, and training. The company is known for its innovative design and marketing, including partnerships with high-profile athletes and teams.

Notable Products

  • Air Jordan sneakers: A line of basketball shoes that were first released in 1985 and have since become a cultural phenomenon.
  • Nike Air Max shoes: A line of running shoes that are known for their visible air cushioning technology.
  • Pegasus running shoes: A line of running shoes that have been popular since the 1980s and are known for their durability and comfort.

Sustainability

Nike is committed to reducing its environmental impact and has set a goal of using only sustainable materials in its products by 2025. The company has also committed to reducing its carbon footprint and water usage.
